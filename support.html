Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control the car?

Use the on-screen joystick on the left side to steer up and down. The right side of the screen has acceleration (tilt right on joystick) and braking (tilt left). Tap the boost button for a speed burst when your boost meter is charged.

How does the leaderboard work?

After a race ends, if your score is high enough you can submit it with a display name of your choice. Leaderboard scores are public and visible to all players.

Can I customize my car?

Yes! Visit the Garage from the main menu to change your car's body color, cabin, wheels, lights, and flame color.

Does the game require an internet connection?

No. You can play offline. An internet connection is only needed to submit and view leaderboard scores.

How do I delete my leaderboard entry?

Open an issue on our GitHub repository (linked below) with the display name and approximate score, and we'll remove it.

The game is running slowly. What can I do?

Try closing other apps running in the background. Rally Racer 2000 runs best on iPhone 8 / iPad (6th gen) or newer with iOS 17+.
